# ContratIA Abierta Product Context

register: product

## Product purpose

ContratIA Abierta helps veedurias, internal-control offices and data journalists
prioritize human review of Colombian public-procurement processes using open SECOP
data, explainable scores and traceable evidence.

## Users

- Data journalist preparing a weekly review queue.
- Citizen oversight analyst checking many public processes.
- Internal-control reviewer who needs evidence before opening a manual review.

## Tone

Technical, sober, accountable and evidence-first. The product never claims wrongdoing,
does not replace legal or fiscal audit, and uses the language of prioritization,
confidence, traceability and human review.

## Anti-references

No generic AI visuals, robots, brains, neon interfaces, hype slogans, stock imagery,
glassmorphism, gradient text or vague "revolutionary" claims.
