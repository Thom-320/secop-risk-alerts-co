# ContratIA Abierta Design Context

## Register

Product UI and technical presentation. Design serves evidence and comprehension.

## Palette

- Canvas: `oklch(0.985 0.004 250)`
- Ink: `oklch(0.22 0.012 250)`
- Muted: `oklch(0.48 0.012 250)`
- Rule: `oklch(0.88 0.008 250)`
- Accent: `oklch(0.46 0.115 245)`
- Accent soft: `oklch(0.95 0.025 245)`

## Typography

Use system sans-serif. Prefer compact, clear hierarchy over display typography.

## Presentation rules

- Light background, restrained accent.
- One idea per slide.
- Large proof objects: screenshots, diagrams, metrics.
- No decorative AI iconography.
- No text walls.
- Speaker notes carry detail.
